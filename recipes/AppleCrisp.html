Apple Crisp

Description

This is a tried and tested apple crust recipe ideal for sharing with friends and family during the fall season

Make sure to have all the ingredients at hand, follow the steps closely and you are guaranteed to have a grand time with this quick and convenient recipe. Easier than apple pie!

Ingredients

    Filling
  • 8 apples
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    Topping
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup oats

Steps

  1. Preheat oven at 177oC
  2. Mix filling ingredients
  3. Make the topping
  4. Bake for 45 minutes
  5. Serve with vanilla ice cream and caramel sauce
  6. Store leftovers in fridge
Enjoy!
