Tomato Soup

Description

A simple and beautiful meal that is sure to warm the cold days

If you are fond of tomatoes like me, this soup will have you wanting to make it over and over again!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 56 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1/4 cup chopped basil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/3 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Saut√© butter and onion for 10 minutes and add garlic
  2. Add tomatoes, chicken stock, basil, sugar and pepper
  3. Bring to a boil and simmer for 10 minutes
  4. Add heavy cream and cheese and simmer
  5. Ladle into bowls and serve with cheese and basil
Enjoy!
