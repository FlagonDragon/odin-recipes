Beef Stroganoff

Description

If it's one of those days you are you crave a really filling and nutritious meal, this recipe will knock you off your feet

Beef, onions, butter, wineâ€” what more could anyone ask for? Delicious is an understatement!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 4 ounces butter
  • 4 green onions
  • 4 tablespoons flour
  • 1 can beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon mustard
  • 6 ounces sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Remove fat and gristle, cut into strips
  2. Melt butter over medium heat and brown beef
  3. Add onions and cook 4 minutes
  4. Pour flour, broth, and stir until boiling
  5. Lower heat, add mustard and simmer for 1 hour
  6. Stir in mushrooms, sour cream and white wine
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve
Enjoy!
